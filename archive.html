The Anonium Archive

The Anonium 21 is a collection of twenty one cinematic artifacts inspired by the history, culture, and mythology of Bitcoin. Some archive files reinterpret historical events. Others explore influential figures within the Bitcoin ecosystem. Some depict speculative futures where decentralized systems face new forms of control.

Archive Overview

Archive Structure

Each film exists as a master inscription preserved on Bitcoin and is accompanied by delegated poster artifacts and additional recovered materials from the archive. Not all recovered materials appear immediately. Some are surfaced later and distributed to collectors preserving poster artifacts.
